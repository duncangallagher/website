about me

Hi, I'm Duncan. I'm a developer with over a decade of experience working on the front-end, and lately I've been working to become full-stack. I love creating joyful and practical user experiences to build brand identity and trust. I'm a native Texan, currently living in Dallas with my wife and our two cats. When not coding, I love traveling, reading, and video editing.
